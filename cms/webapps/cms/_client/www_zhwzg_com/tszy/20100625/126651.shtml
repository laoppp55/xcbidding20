功效与特点：
益肝冲剂具有活血化瘀，行气健脾，解毒利胆的功效。其作用特点：
1、抗肿瘤作用。本品所含丹参酮类、郁金挥发油、姜黄素、当归多糖、牡丹皮提取物、白术多糖、内酯、陈皮橘类黄酮、厚朴酚、和厚朴酚、三棱、山楂中的槲皮素等中药有效成分有抑制肿瘤生长的作用，主要用于辅助治疗肝癌、胰腺癌、胆囊癌、胆管癌。
2、肿瘤并发症的治疗。用于治疗因肿瘤引起的黄疸和腹水。
3、抗病毒作用。临床用于急、慢性肝炎，肝硬化等的治疗。

Efficacy and Characteristics：
Yigan Chongji activates blood circulation, removes stasis, promotes qi circulation, strengthens the spleen, removes toxins and benefits the gallbladder. It is characterized as:
1. Antitumor. The effective components contained in the product, such as tanshinones, essential oil from Radix Curcumae Aromaticae, curcumin, angelica sinensis polysaccharides, cortex moutan extract, atractylodes macrocephala koidz polysaccharide, lactones, citrus bioflavonoids of Pericarpium Citri Reticulatae, magnolo, honokiol, and quercetin in Rhizoma Sparganii and Fructus Crataegi, inhibit growth of tumor and it is mainly used as adjuvant therapy for carcinoma of liver, pancreas, gallbladder and biliary duct.
2. Treatment of tumor complication. It is used for treatment of jaundice and asciatis induced by tumor.
3. Antivirus. It is used for acute and chronic hepatitis, hepatic sclerosis, etc.
