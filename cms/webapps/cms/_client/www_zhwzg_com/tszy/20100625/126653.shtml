功效与特点：
骨炎灵片具有补益气血，清热解毒，消肿止痛的功效。其作用特点：
1、抗肿瘤作用。本品所含黄芪、地黄、川芎、枸杞子、甘草、白芷、蒲公英等中药有抑制肿瘤生长的作用，对骨瘤及骨转移有辅助治疗的作用。
2、促进被破坏的骨质愈合。骨碎补能促进骨对钙的吸收，并能提高血钙和血磷的水平，从而有利于被破坏骨质的修复。
3、增强免疫功能。经现代药理学研究表明：本品所含当归、黄芪、地黄、菟丝子等成分具有明显增强免疫功能的作用。
4、化疗减毒及放射增敏作用。枸杞子具有增敏减毒作用，枸杞子对lewis肺癌有明显的放疗增敏作用。对放化疗后导致的久泻不止有较好的止泻作用。

Efficacy and Characteristics：
Gu Yan Ling Pian acts on tonifying qi and blood, clearing away heat, detoxification, removing swelling and stopping pain. It is characterized as:
1. Antitumor. The product contains Huangqi (Radix Astragali), Dihuang (Radix Rehmanniae), Chuanxiong (Rhizoma Chuanxiong), Gouqizi (Fructus Lyeii), Gancao (Glycyrrhizae Uralensis), Baizhi (Angelica Dahurica), Pugongying (Taraxacum Mongolicum Hand-mazz), etc. It suppresses tumor growth and has adjuvant action on bone carcinoma and bone metastasis.
2. Enhance healing of destroyed bone. Gusuibu (Davallia Barometz) acts on promoting the absorption of calcium in bone and improve Ca and P levels in blood so that it benefits the repair of destroyed bone.
3. Enhance immunity. Modern pharmacological study indicates that the components of the product, such as Danggui (Angelica Snensis), Huangqi (Radix Astragali), Dihuang (Radix Rehmanniae) and Tusizi (Semen Cuscutae), enhance apparently the immunity.
4. Detoxification in chemotherapy and sensibilization in radiotherapy. Gouqizi (Fructus Lyeii) acts on sensibilization and detoxification and is apparently on sensibilization in radiotherapy for lewis pulmonary carcinoma and can stop chronic diarrhea well induced by chemoradiotherapy.
