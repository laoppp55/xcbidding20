　功效与特点：
　鹤蟾片具有解毒除痰，凉血祛痰，消Y散结的功效，是具有显著疗效的广谱抗肿瘤药物。其作用特点：
　　1、抑制肿瘤生长。本品中所含干蟾皮、仙鹤草、人参、半夏、葶苈子、浙贝母、鱼腥草、猫爪草等，除用于治疗呼吸系统肿瘤（如肺癌、喉癌、鼻咽癌及肺转移癌）和纵隔部位的肿瘤外，临床研究表明其对脑肿瘤、骨肿瘤、肝癌、胆囊癌、胰腺癌、肾癌、膀胱癌、前列腺癌、恶性淋巴瘤、甲状腺癌、白血病及红斑狼疮等均有明显疗效。
　　2、提高机体免疫功能。本品所含的仙鹤草、人参、半夏、鱼腥草等药物成分能够有效的提高体质，改善患者恶液质。


He Chan Pian

【Name of Drug】He Chan Pian

　Efficacy and Characteristics：
　He Chan Pian acts on detoxification, resolving phlegm, cooling blood and removing masses. It has significant efficacy on antitumor of wide spectrum in clinic. It is characterized as,
　　1. Inhibit tumor growth. This product contains Ganchanpi (Skin of Bufo bufo Gargarizans), Xianhecao (Agrimonia Pilosa Ledeb), Renshen (Panax Ginseng), Shengbanxia (Unprocessed Rhizoma Pinelliae), Tinglizi (Semen Rescurainiae), Zhebeimu (Fritillaria Thunbergii), Yuxingcao (Houttuynia Cordata Thunb.), Maozhuacao (Radix Ranunculi Ternati), etc. Except tumor in respiratory system (such as lung carcinoma, laryngeal carcinoma, nasopharynx carcinoma and pulmonary metastatic carcinoma) and mediastinal region, clinical study indicates that this drug has apparent efficacy on carcinoma of brain, bone, liver, gallbladder, pancreas, kidney, urinary bladder and pancreas; malignant lymphoma, thyroid carcinoma, leukemia, lupus erythematosus, etc.
　　2. Enhance immunity. Xianhecao (Agrimonia Pilosa Ledeb), Renshen (Panax Ginseng), Shengbanxia (Unprocessed Rhizoma Pinelliae) contained in the product can enhance immunity and relieve cachexia effectively.
