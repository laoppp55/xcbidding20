【性 状】 本品为红棕色的液体；味甜、微酸涩。
【功能主治】 健脾补肾。适用于神疲乏力，腰膝酸软，头晕目眩者。
【规　　格】 10ml×6支／盒

5、服药2周症状无缓解，应去医院就诊。
6、对本品过敏者禁用，过敏体质者慎用。
7、本品性状发生改变时禁止使用。
8、请将本品放在儿童不能接触的地方。

　功效与特点：
　　1、调节免疫功能。本品中所含的人参、首乌、黄芪、五味子、枸杞及甘草等中药具有健脾补肾的功效，可促进T细胞增殖、提高吞噬细胞的吞噬功能、增高免疫球蛋白的含量等，因此有效增强了机体的免疫功能。
　　2、减轻放化疗毒副作用。本品可保护造血机能，防止白细胞降低，减轻放化疗导致的恶心呕吐及脱发等症状。
　　3、抗衰老。本品所含的黄芪、五味子可有效的清除生物体新陈代谢所产生的超氧阴离子自由基，改善体内环境，达到延缓衰老的作用。
4、抗疲劳。本品可调节物质代谢及神经-内分泌系统功能、快速有效的达到解除疲劳感的作用。
　　5、本品所含中药成分有祛斑、减皱、美容、乌发的功效。


Shen Wei Kou Fu Ye
【Name of Drug】 Shen Wei Kou Fu Ye
【Ingredients】 Renshen (Panax Ginseng), Huangqi (Radix Astragali), Gouqizi (Lycii Chinensis), Heshouwu (Polygonum Multiflorum), Wuweizi (Schizandrae Chinensis), Baihuasheshecao (Hedyotis Diffusae Willd), Tianhuafen (Radix Trichosanthis), Weilingxian (Clematis Chinensis Osbeck), Gancao (Glycyrrhizae uralensis). Excipients: honey, sorbate
【Characteristics】This product is red brown liquid, sweet, slightly sour and astringent in flavor.
【Function and Indication】 Strengthen the spleen and kidney. It is applied to the treatment of lassitude, soreness and weakness of lumbar region and knee joints, dizziness and vertigo.
【Specification】 10ml ×6pcs/box
【Usage and Dosage】 Oral application, 10ml each time, 2~3 times a day
　【Precautions】
　　1. Avoid spicy, raw, cold and greasy food.
　　2. Not suitable for common cold and fever, use cautiously for women in lactation.
　　3. Take before meal. Take after shaking if with sedimentation.
　　4. Take under the guide of doctor for patient with chronic diseases, such as hypertension, heart disease, liver disease, diabetes, and kidney disease.
　　5. See a doctor if no release of symptoms after 2-week medication
　　6. Prohibit for patient allergic to the product and use cautiously for the one of allergic constitution.
　　7. Prohibit to use when the characteristics of the product changes.
　　8. Please place the product far from children.
　　9. Please consult doctor or pharmacist before using the product if other drugs are being taken.
【Storage】 Sealing in cool and dry place
【Validity】 24 months
